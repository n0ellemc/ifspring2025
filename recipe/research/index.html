Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Here's a basic choclate chip cookie recipe, largely sourced from joyfoodsunshine.com:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup salted butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 cups of flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Â½ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips (12 oz)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F, lay out baking sheet
  2. In a medium bowl mix flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt
  3. Cream together butter and sugars until combined
  4. Beat in eggs and vanilla until light
  5. Mix in the dry ingredients until combined
  6. Add chocolate chips and mix well
  7. Roll 2-3 Tablespoons of dough and place on baking sheet
  8. Bake in oven for 8-10 minutes
  9. Let sit for 5 minutes and enjoy!
